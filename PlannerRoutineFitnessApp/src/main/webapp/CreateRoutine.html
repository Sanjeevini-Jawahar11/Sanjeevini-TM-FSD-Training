Choose YOUR OWN WorkOut!!!
                        Circuits can be a great way to work out and
                reduce stress without any special equipment

                             TO BUILD YOUR CIRCUIT, CHOOSE 3-4
                             EXCERCISE FROM EACH CATEGORY:
     
                              Alternate Cardio and strength exercises in
                             short bursts of 30 seconds for up to 3 minutes,
                              then repeat the circuit two to three times.


  
CARDIO EXERCISE
  • Jumping jacks
  • Squat jumps
  • Jogging or Marching in place
  • Stair-Climbing or step-ups
  • High Knees
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Star jumps
  • Burpees
STRENGTH EXERCISE
  • Plank and side plank
  • PushUps
  • Sit-ups or crunches
  • Hiplift or bridge position
  • Tricep dips on a chair
  • Lunges
  • Squats or chair position
  • Wall sits
FLEXIBILITY EXERCISE
  • Standing Quad Stretch
  • Seat Straddle Lotus
  • Seat Side Straddle
  • Seat Stretch
  • Knees to Chest
  • Side Lunges
  • Cross-Over
  • Forward Lunges
